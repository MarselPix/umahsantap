## üì¶ **DATABASE DESIGN (UPDATE)**  

### **Tabel `menu`**  
- id_menu (INT, AUTO_INCREMENT, PRIMARY KEY)  
- nama_menu (VARCHAR)  
- kategori (ENUM('makanan','minuman'))  
- harga (INT)  
- gambar (VARCHAR)  
- stok (INT)  
- rating (FLOAT)  

### **Tabel `pesanan`**  
- id_pesanan (INT, AUTO_INCREMENT, PRIMARY KEY)  
- nama_pemesan (VARCHAR)  
- no_hp (VARCHAR)  
- alamat (TEXT)  
- metode_bayar (ENUM('COD','BRI','DANA','SHOPEEPAY'))  
- status (ENUM('dipesan','diproses','diantar','selesai'))  
- total_harga (INT)  
- tanggal_pesan (DATETIME)  

### **Tabel `detail_pesanan`**  
- id_detail (INT, AUTO_INCREMENT, PRIMARY KEY)  
- id_pesanan (INT, FOREIGN KEY)  
- id_menu (INT, FOREIGN KEY)  
- jumlah (INT)  
- subtotal (INT)  

### **Tabel `admin`**  
- id_admin (INT, AUTO_INCREMENT, PRIMARY KEY)  
- username (VARCHAR)  
- password (VARCHAR) (MD5)
